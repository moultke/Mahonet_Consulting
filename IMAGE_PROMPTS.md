## General Guidelines for All Images:

1. **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 (landscape)
2. **Resolution:** Minimum 1280x720, ideally 1920x1080
3. **Style:** Professional photography, photorealistic
4. **Lighting:** Natural, warm, professional
5. **Color Palette:**
   - Primary: Natural tones with good lighting
   - Accent: Orange (#FF6B35) and Eggplant (#571326)
6. **Diversity:** Include diverse individuals in all images
7. **Mood:** Professional, warm, optimistic, trustworthy
8. **Composition:** Well-lit, clean, modern, not too busy

## Recommended AI Tools:

- **Midjourney** (best for photorealistic professional images)
- **DALL-E 3** (via ChatGPT Plus)
- **Adobe Firefly** (great for commercial use)
- **Stable Diffusion** (via DreamStudio or local)
- **Leonardo.ai** (good for professional photography style)

## After Generating Images:

1. Save each image with the filename specified above
2. Place all three images in the `img/` folder
3. Images will automatically display on the homepage in the "Who We Serve" section
4. Ensure images are under 500KB each for optimal web performance (use tinypng.com if needed)
