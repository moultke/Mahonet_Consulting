## Tips for Best Results

### For AI Generation:
1. **Start with Midjourney or DALL-E 3** for best photorealistic results
2. **Add "--ar 16:9"** at the end of Midjourney prompts for correct aspect ratio
3. **Generate 4-5 variations** of each and pick the best
4. **Refine the prompt** if the first results aren't quite right
5. **Check licensing** - most AI tools allow commercial use, but verify

### For Stock Photos:
1. **Search multiple sites** to find the perfect match
2. **Look for diversity** in the people shown
3. **Avoid overly staged** or "stock photo looking" images
4. **Check the license** - ensure commercial use is allowed
5. **Download highest resolution** available

### General:
- **Keep it authentic** - real-looking people, not overly posed
- **Warm and professional** - convey trust and expertise
- **Include diversity** - represent your actual target audiences
- **Match the vibe** - behavioral health = compassionate, small business = confident, nonprofits = energetic
