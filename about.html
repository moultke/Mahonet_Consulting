About Us

At MHRC, we partner with small businesses and behavioral health agencies to simplify compliance, strengthen leadership, and build confident, people-first workplaces. Our approach combines strategic insight with real-world HR experience, helping clients stay compliant while creating environments where employees can thrive.

Most small businesses don't see that their growing pains are HR pains — we help you uncover the cause, strengthen your systems, and empower your team to thrive.

Meet Terris

Founder & HR Director, MHRC

With almost two decades of experience in corporate HR, Terris saw how easily people can get lost in the shuffle — especially in large, one-size-fits-all environments. When her role was outsourced, she didn't see it as an ending, but as the beginning of something more personal, intentional, and impactful.

That experience inspired her to create MHRC, with one mission: to help small businesses thrive through strategic, compassionate, and compliance-driven HR support.

Terris is a connector, a builder, and a believer in people. She understands that HR isn't just about policies — it's about relationships, culture, and creating structure that allows both businesses and teams to grow.

Through MHRC, she combines corporate expertise with a people-first approach, helping small businesses and behavioral health agencies bridge the gap between compliance and care.

When she's not supporting clients, Terris enjoys cycling, traveling with her family, and finding new ways to help leaders grow with confidence.
