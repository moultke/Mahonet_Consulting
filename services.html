Our Services

Strategic HR Support Rooted in Compliance and Care

MHRC provides the expertise and structure your organization needs to grow with confidence. We go beyond quick fixes and surface-level advice — delivering hands-on support that strengthens your team, protects your business, and builds lasting systems for success.

🗂️

HR Compliance & Audits

Stay audit-ready and confident.

We review your policies, procedures, and documentation to ensure alignment with state, federal, and accreditation requirements — including CARF and OHMAS. You'll receive clear recommendations, practical action steps, and the support to implement them.

What this includes:

  • Compliance risk assessments
  • Policy and procedure reviews
  • Personnel file audits
  • Accreditation readiness support
  • Compliance training for leaders
👥

Recruiting & Talent Acquisition

Find and keep the right people.

We help you attract qualified, reliable candidates who fit your mission and culture — from job postings to onboarding. Our structured, compliance-friendly recruiting process saves time and reduces turnover.

What this includes:

  • Job descriptions and postings
  • Interview questions and scoring guides
  • Candidate screening support
  • Hiring compliance (I-9, references, background)
  • New hire onboarding structure
📘

Policy & Employee Handbooks

Protect your business — and your people.

A strong handbook sets the tone for your workplace and protects against risk. We create or update policies that are compliant, easy to understand, and customized to your organization's needs.

What this includes:

  • New or updated employee handbooks
  • Policy writing and formatting
  • Timekeeping, attendance, and PTO policies
  • Acknowledgment forms and rollout guidance
🤝

Employee Relations & Investigations

Resolve issues with professionalism and care.

When challenges arise, we guide you through them with discretion and fairness. Our approach helps you address concerns promptly while maintaining compliance and workplace integrity.

What this includes:

  • Employee complaint response
  • Investigations and documentation
  • Coaching and corrective action guidance
  • Supervisor consultation and follow-up strategy
🎯

Leadership Coaching & Training

Develop confident, capable leaders.

We help supervisors and managers strengthen communication, accountability, and team performance. Through one-on-one coaching or group sessions, we build leaders who elevate culture and drive results.

What this includes:

  • Supervisor and site-leader training
  • Coaching for new or emerging leaders
  • Performance management support
  • Team culture and engagement sessions
